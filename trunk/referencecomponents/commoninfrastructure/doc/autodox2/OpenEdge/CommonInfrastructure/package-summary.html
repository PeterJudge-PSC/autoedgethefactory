Package Description

Common Infrastructure services are non-domain specific related functions that provide the common infrastructure support for a modern application. They comprise of standard behavior, features and functionality independent of any specific application requirements. Their combined presence determines the nature and style of applications supported.

The OpenEdge.CommonInfrastructure package holds components of general nature that are used in more than one of the OERA layers, and could potentially be used by other ABL applications.
